Delivery Models

01

Design-Build

The design knows how it gets made from the first sketch. No translation loss between drawing and jobsite. Fewer change orders, because the people solving the problem are the people pouring the concrete and running the CNC.

"On IMTC, we put dowels in the freezer to fit them into curved DLT panels. That's not in any manual — it's creative common sense from a team that designs and builds."

02

Value Engineering + Build

We bring 27 years of attention to detail to every connection, every span, every fabrication step. We've cut 40% off structures — not because the original engineer was wrong, but because they didn't build. Building teaches you what's necessary and what's excess.

"On Peel Police HQ, the original design was oversized by 40%. The owner made them accept our redesign line by line. Lighter, faster, less money."

03

Consulting

Feasibility, material selection, specific engineering problems. No shop time required — just expansive curiosity from people who've worked across timber, steel, concrete, and hybrid systems for 27 years.

"A structural engineer in Michigan emailed: 'I want to bring StructureCraft on as a design partner, but I'm struggling with competitive bid requirements.' That's exactly the conversation we love having."

Design is 2% of cost. It determines 80%.

When the designers also build, that 2% works harder. A few weeks of rethinking saves months and millions.
