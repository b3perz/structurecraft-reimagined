Delivery Models

01

Design-Build

When we're your Engineer of Record and your builder, the design knows how it will be made from the first sketch. No translation between disciplines. No information lost between design and construction. The result: faster timelines, fewer change orders, and structures that are both more beautiful and more efficient because they were conceived as buildable from day one.

"On the IMTC Pavilion, we put dowels in the freezer to make them fit into curved DLT panels. That's not a detail you plan for in a traditional process — it's a solution that comes from designing and building being the same team."

02

Value Engineering + Build

When another engineer has designed it, we bring 25 years of "what if we..." to every connection, every span, every fabrication sequence. We've redesigned structures that were overbuilt by 40% — not because the original engineer was wrong, but because they didn't build. Building teaches you what's necessary and what's excess. We know, because we lift every beam.

"On Peel Police HQ, the original engineer's design was oversized by 40%. The owner made them accept our redesign line by line. The building was lighter, faster to construct, and cost dramatically less."

03

Consulting

Sometimes you just need the smartest timber brain in the room. We consult on feasibility, advise on material selection, solve specific structural challenges, and help architects and engineers who are new to mass timber find their footing. No shop time required — just knowledge earned from 27 years of designing and building.

"A structural engineer in Michigan emailed us: 'I want to bring StructureCraft on as a design partner, but I'm struggling to understand how we can bring in design-build on a project that has to be competitively bid.' That's the conversation we love having."
