Why we publish this

Nobody in our industry publishes efficiency metrics. We think that should change.

When you see a beautiful timber building, it's natural to assume it was expensive. Sometimes it is. Often, it isn't — because the structure was designed by people who also build, who know exactly how a beam will be lifted, how a connection will be fabricated, how to eliminate waste before it's ever created.

We share these numbers not to tear anyone down, but to show what's possible when engineering, design, and construction think as one.
