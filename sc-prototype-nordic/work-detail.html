Gaylord Rockies Pool

Aurora, Colorado · 2019

42m

Span

14 months

Timeline

Significant

Cost reduction

38%

Less material

The original engineer's design was over budget. The contractor was considering switching to steel — which would have changed the character of the space entirely. Wood above water has a warmth that steel doesn't.

We looked at the structure itself. What if the ceiling geometry wasn't decorative but followed the most efficient way to span 42 meters with glulam? We worked through the loads, the connections, the fabrication sequence. It took a couple of weeks to find the answer.

The result used considerably less material, came in on budget, and — we think — looks better for it. The curves aren't decoration. They're the shape the forces want to take.
