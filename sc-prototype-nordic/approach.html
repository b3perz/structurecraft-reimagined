Delivery Models

01

Design-Build

The design knows how it gets made from the first sketch. Fewer change orders, because the people solving the problem are the same ones on the jobsite.

On the IMTC pavilion, we had to freeze dowels to fit them into curved DLT panels. That kind of thinking comes from a team that designs and builds together.

02

Value Engineering + Build

Another engineer designed it, we build it. Years of hands-on experience tend to reveal where structures can be simplified — not because the original was wrong, but because building teaches you things that drawing alone doesn't.

On Peel Police HQ, the original design had room to be lighter. The owner asked us to work through it detail by detail.

03

Consulting

Feasibility, material selection, specific engineering questions. No shop time required — just careful thinking from people who've worked across timber, steel, concrete, and hybrid systems.

A structural engineer reached out about bringing us on as a design partner. That's exactly the kind of conversation we enjoy.

Design is a small fraction of cost. It shapes most of it.

When the designers also build, that fraction works harder. A few weeks of rethinking can save months in the field.
